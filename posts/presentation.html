Hello, Thank you for visiting my page! My name is Erick Mendieta I am a computer programmer who is now learning Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing at Tokyo Institute of Technology in Japan. I have been living in Tokyo for a little more than a year. I come from Costa Rica, a little country in Central America. Pura Vida! Here are some pictures of Costa Rica!



I want to start this post by sharing one of the quotes that I like the most by Isaac Asimov. Asimov's stories heavily influenced my childhood; When I was a kid my father gave me the first book I ever read "Fundation" by Isaac Asimov. I certainly was amaze by the stories of distance civilizations, psychohistorians and mathematical models that can determine the fate of the human race. With the time I started to become more interested in the topic and read the robot series right before the movie "Bicentennial man" was released.

The true delight is in the finding out rather than in the knowing. Isaac Asimov
